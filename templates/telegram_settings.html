Setup Instructions

  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot to create a new bot
  3. Follow the instructions and copy the Bot Token
  4. Search for @userinfobot and send /start
  5. Copy your Chat ID (it will be a number)
  6. Start a conversation with your new bot (send /start)
  7. Fill in the form above and click Save Settings
  8. Click Test Connection to verify

Notification Types

Schedule Created: When a stream is successfully scheduled to YouTube
Stream Starting: When a scheduled stream goes live
Stream Ended: When a stream finishes
Errors: When there's an error creating a schedule
